ABOUT US

We are Kevin and Naomi along with our little dog, Dilys.

Our story starts when we first met in 2014 and found out we're traveling to the same internship in New Zealand. We knew from the get go that we both enjoyed Travelling, considering the coincidence of us both going to New Zealand. Since then we have been eager to go to as many places as possible. We went to Iceland, Greece, Canary islands, the Netherlands (Kevin is dutch!) Dominican republic.
After work and University commitments we decided to actually try to make the dream of Travelling a reality. Then covid hit, so we thought it would be the perfect time to buy a van and make it into a home so we can go to as many places as possible. The van build took us a lot longer than expected and so many bumps in the road.
WE DID IT!
We know this won't come without a few more challenges, but that's all part of the fun. March 2023 we are planning our big trip to hit off as many countries as possible. Not only can you read all about our van adventures on here, but you can also watch us on YouTube (INSERT URL).
We are so happy to be able to share this journey with you to join us on our journey.

Lots of love, Kevin, Naomi, Dilys and Wilson xx
